Kuamo Expeditions (Colombia)

Kuamo

Kuamo is a Colombian company dedicated to Tourism, Expeditions, and Organizing Sporting Events.

The task was to create a Logo, a Brand Manual, and Corporate Business Cards for this venture.

In the Kankuamo language, Kuamo translates to "Guardians of the World's Balance".

Inspired by Los Andes

The icon is inspired by the majestic Andes mountain range of Colombia, surrounded by a semicircle, symbolizing protection and balance.

"Having a brand that represents the places we inhabit makes us proud of what we do."

Felipe Mallea / Founder Kuamo
