LEANDRO FUENZALIDA

UX/UI Designer & no-code developer

As a visual designer, I have been working in this field for about 12 years, gaining knowledge and experience in editorials, digital media, and providing brand identity consultancy to both entrepreneurs and technology companies with a local and global presence

This website serves as my portfolio, built using HTML5 + CSS & Javascript, and its repository can be found on my Github Profile. Here, you will find a diverse collection of digital and print pieces, including landing pages, presentations, digital campaigns, mailing campaigns, user interfaces, logos, and graphic materials for international architecture competitions, technology companies, and strategic initiatives in Santiago de Chile.

In 2012, I graduated with a Diploma in Web Design from Academia Mac. This program gave me a strong foundation in web and digital design, particularly focusing on the User-Centered Design (UCD) approach

To further develop my expertise in this area, I completed a Diploma in User Experience and Usability from Universidad Católica in 2018. This solidified my skills in the field.

On top of that, I honed my abilities in Customer Journey mapping, prioritization tables, Lean UX methodology, and storytelling.

From 2022 to 2024, I had the opportunity to collaborate with the Growth Marketing team at ServiceRocket as a UX/UI & Webflow Developer. My experience there was enriching. I learned about their services and products to implement design procedures for creating Touchpoints/Landing pages. Additionally, I focused on continuous improvement using heatmaps and analytics.
