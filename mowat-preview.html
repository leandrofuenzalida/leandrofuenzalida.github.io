BIOGRAPHY

Composer, singer-songwriter and record producer from Chile. Nominated as best record producer in 2016, 2019 and 2022 at the Pulsar Awards. Between 2020 and 2022 he composed the original soundtrack for the Spotify Original Audio Series "Case 63“ released worldwide with different versions for the english speaking market (CASE 63), Latin American and Spanish market (CASO 63), as well as adaptations for Brazil (PACIENTE 63) and India (VIRUS 2062). He has released 3 albums of his own music and has worked as record producer with people like Leo Quinteros, Kat Frankie, Leo Saavedra, Erik Penny, (me llamo) Sebastián, David Gaffney, Antonio Del Favero, Colombina Parra, Diego Peralta, Chinoy, Cadenasso, Fernando Milagros and Tenemos Explosivos.
